Key Features

Integrated Knowledge Base

The evaluator is also a learning tool. For each criterion, you will find two help icons:

  • Criterion Info: Explains the objective, benefits, and provides a practical example.
  • Market Solutions: Shows you real materials and manufacturers in Portugal to meet the credit requirements.

Advanced Tools

Life Cycle Cost Analysis (LCCA)

Justify your sustainable choices economically. This calculator, integrated into the "Market Solutions," estimates the total cost of a material over its life cycle, considering initial investment, maintenance, and energy savings.

How to access: Click the Market Solutions icon for a relevant criterion and look for the "Analyze Life Cycle Cost" button.

Descriptive Report Generator

Automatically generate a preliminary descriptive report based on the credits you select. This text can be copied and used as a starting point for your project's official documentation, saving you valuable time.
